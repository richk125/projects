Banana Split

A banana split is a delightful and indulgent ice cream dessert that combines a medley of flavors and textures. It begins with a perfectly ripe banana, which is split lengthwise and nestled in a dish. Resting on the banana are three scoops of creamy, flavorful ice cream typically vanilla, chocolate, and strawberry arranged side by side. Each scoop is draped with its own luscious topping. The dish is then crowned with a generous dollop of whipped cream, which is often adorned with colorful sprinkles or crushed nuts. Maraschino cherries are placed atop each scoop. The result is a decadent and visually appealing treat that combines the creamy richness of ice cream, the smoothness of the sauces, and the satisfying crunch of the toppings, all harmonized by the fresh, fruity banana base. Whether enjoyed as a nostalgic favorite or a special indulgence, a banana split offers a delightful explosion of flavors and textures in every bite.

Ingredients

  • 1 scoop chocolate ice cream
  • 2 tablespoons chocolate syrup
  • 1 scoop vanilla ice cream
  • 2 tablespoons crushed sweetened pineapple
  • 1 scoop strawberry ice cream
  • 2 tablespoons strawberry compote
  • Whipping cream
  • 1 Banana
  • Maraschino cherries
  • Peanuts or walnuts

Steps

  1. Peel and slice a banana half lengthwise.
  2. Place one piece of the banana on each side of the dish.
  3. Place a scoop of vanilla, chocolate and strawberry ice cream between the banana pieces.
  4. Place crushed pineapple on top of the vanilla ice cream.
  5. Place the strawberry compote on top of the strawberry ice cream.
  6. Place chocolate syrup on top of the chocolate ice cream.
  7. Top each ice cream scoop with whipped cream.
  8. Place three maraschino cherries on top, one in each whipped cream pile.
  9. Sprinkle with peanuts or walnuts.
